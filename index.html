Generative Adversarial Network

After reading a few papers on generative adversarial networks, I learned how GAN's can be used to generate tabular data. Using The spotify API, I used the company's music analysis data from the songs of the the top house and techno artists to train a discriminator and generator set of networks that transforms a noise vector into a completely new musical rhythm tensor. I then created a method to transform that tensor into music midi files, which are usable in a drum machine within a digital audio workstation.

Natural Language Processing

In this project, I decided to create a classification model with a realistic use case. If Reddit wanted to increase user engagement, one method they could employ is to introduce users to subreddits that they could also be interested in. As an introductory proof of concept, I created a binary predictive model that attempts to predict which subreddit a post belongs to. I chose r/science and r/space, and employed logistic regression, KNN, and random forest classification methods.

Multivariable Regression

In a Kaggle competition, I am tasked with determining the relationship between a large set of variables and single family housing prices around Ames, Iowa. This analysis will involve collecting and analyzing data on various factors that may affect housing prices, such as neighborhood, size, and age of the properties. The ultimate goal is to provide a theoretical with insights on how these factors impact housing prices in the area, and to identify potential opportunities for investment in the single family housing market around Ames, Iowa. In this project I employ traditional linear regression methods, and additionally feature-minimizing methods such as lasso and ridge regression.
